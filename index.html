Our Story

Founded in 2025, Empower Her Africa is a grassroots organization dedicated to creating sustainable change for girls and women in underserved communities across Kenya.

We believe that when you empower a woman, you empower an entire community. Our holistic approach addresses education gaps, health disparities, and economic barriers that keep women from reaching their full potential.

Through our various programs, we've reached over 5,000 women and girls, helping them break free from cycles of poverty and build brighter futures.
